Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Privacy Commitment

FC Clubs App is designed with a fundamental respect for your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

Data Collection & Usage

  • Local Storage: All your club data, match history, and squad details are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
  • Direct Sync: The app communicates directly with EA Sports servers to retrieve your club's statistics. We do not intercept or store this data.
  • Anonymous Analytics: We use Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics) to identify crashes and improve app stability. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for anonymous crash reporting and analytics.

Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at support@fcclubs.app.
